Box Office Blight

Major studios cut theatrical releases as post-pandemic ticket sales slow recovery

By Adam Hudacek

The Covid-19 pandemic threatened to wipe out the theatrical distribution industry, but in the years since lockdown ended, recovery has been rapid. However, the overall growth of ticket sales has slowed and failed to meet pre-pandemic levels. Over the same period, major studios have slimmed their release schedules, reducing big-budget products for theater while the indie market, inhabited by minor studios, has surpassed their 2019 numbers.
